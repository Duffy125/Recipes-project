Greek Salad Recipe

Never enought salad recipes right? This delicious and healthy salad is abreeze to make. It just requires some simple chopoing and quick coating of Greek dressing. For a undbeliveable salad that anyone can enjoy.

How to make Greek salad

Greek salad dressing

Directions

salad
  • 4 medium Roma tomatoes, diced (10oz)
  • 1 meduim english cucumber, optionally peeled and sliced into half moons
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced or diced, rinsed and drained
  • 1 small green or yellow bell pepper, seeds and ribs removed, chopped
  • 3/4 cup kalamata or balck olives, drained and sliced
  • 4 oz. crumbled feta cheese (about 1 cup)
  • 1 medium avocado (not to soft),diced
Dressing
  • 6 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp minced garlic (1 large clove)
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp finely miced fresh parsley
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 3/4 tsp honey
  • salt, to taste
Instructions
  1. Add all of the dressing ingredinets to a small mixing bowl and whisk well to blend, season with salt to taste and store in refrigerator until ready to use.
  2. Add tomatoes, Cucumber, onion, bell pepper, olives, avocado and feta to a salad bowl
  3. Drizzle dressing over top and gently toss to evenly coat. Add dressing with in a few minutes of serving for best results.
