Fresh maind naan Greek style Pizza

These ingredients make for a simple yet delicious tasting pizza. The naan bread makes it simple to take an Italian dish and turn it greek.

  • Naan Bread
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Tzatziki
  • Greek-seasoned Chicken Breasts
  • Feta
  • Spinach Leaves
  • Kalamata Olives

How to make the amazing naan pizza

  1. First, preheat the oven to 400F.
  2. Heat a skillet on medium heat with 1 tabelspoon of olive oil. Place your chicken breasts on the skillet and season up-facing side of chicken with 1 tablespoon of greek seasoning. After 4 minutes of cooking, flip the chicken and repeat with the seasoning. Cook until internal temperature of chicken is 165F. Remove from heat, cut into bite sized pieces and set aside.
  3. With your naan bread on a flat surface, spread 2-3 TBS of tzatiki on the top. Next, place about 1/2 Cup of diced chicken on top. follow with 1/4th cup diced spinach leafs, 2 TBS Kalamta olives and 1/4th cup of cherry tomatoes, cut in half length-wise. Top with 3/4 cup of feta.
  4. Place the Pizza on a baking sheet, and bake for 10-12 minutes.Then remove and let sit for about 1 minutes and then enjoy.
